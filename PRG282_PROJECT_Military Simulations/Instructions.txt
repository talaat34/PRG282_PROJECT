The aim is to destroy the enemies camp without out loosing your health or fuel


Movement:

User should use the the directional keys to move the aircraft.
Up - Moves the Aircraft Upwards
Down - Moves the Aircraft Downwards
Left - Moves the Aircraft to the Left
Right - Moves the Aircraft to the Right

Fire:

Space Bar - Shoots bullets from the Aircraft

Restart Game:

Once the Aircrafts Health reaches zero, The simulation needs to restarted manually by the user
Enter - Resets the simulation
